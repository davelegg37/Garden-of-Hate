The Garden of Hate or Love ?? Your goal in this game is to grow more flowers of love than of hate i.e. to achieve a low hate index To  do this you must fertilize your flowers by giving up your beliefs; something very hard to do.The more beliefs you give up the less susceptible you are to the causes of hate and the greater your chances of growing a flower of  love then of hate.. At the beginiing of the game you are asked to select the strength with which you hold your beliefs. Weakly held beliefs are easier to give up then stronger ones. Try out all levels, not just to win but for the insights you might gain. The game ends when you have run out beliefs to give up or your garden fully grown and all your flowers of love have bloomed. Good luck with your gardening
